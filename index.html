Welcome to Scribel!

Translate, Transcribe, Transform

Our website allows people to upload audio and in return get a transcript of the audio.
We also have a translation feature that will change the language this page is viewed in for people who speak other languages or want to learn new languages.
We decided to build this website to make technology more accessible for people hard of hearing and anyone who wants audio to help them pay attention during online classes or to transcribe virtual meetings.
We want to help individuals feel closer together through the magic of sounds and language!

Individuals whose first language is not English are highly encouraged to translate this page into their preferred language!

Transcribe

To transcribe a file, please select "Choose Files" and choose either a wav or mp3 audio file. Once you've done this, please click "Start recognition" to transcribe it. You may only select one input at a time. When you transcribe a different file, it will automatically reset the output box. Make sure you are picking the language the file is in or the transcription will be wrong.
